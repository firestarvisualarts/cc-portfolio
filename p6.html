Data Portrait

Description

Our task was to create a data portrait of a habit of ours. We were asked to track a habit, translate it into an array, and then animate it using any concept we learned prior to this assignment.

Design Process

I chose to track how many times I fed my cat Sarge. I tracked from 10/05/2021 to 10/11/2021. An entire week. Whenever I fed him, I listed the times in a Word document. In code, I chose to represent the actual times as circles. The legend is under the map.

Reflection

I learned that the amount of times I feed Sarge depends on how much I'm home. Since half my classes are in person, I'm not home nearly as much as I was last year. This past weekend was unusually busy, as Comic Con was in town, so I wasn't home as much as I usually am on weekends. My cat has his own internal clock, so he's usually waking me up around 7 AM for food. If I don't have to wake up early that day, I go back to bed after feeding him. As a student, I go to bed really late, so his last meal of the day is usually around midnight or 1 AM. Saturday amd Sunday should have a few more points, but my mom was visiting, so she took some of the responsibility of feeding him.

I made a list of times, and grouped them together. I wanted them to correspond with the 6 colors of the rainbow, so I made 6 groups, using the 24 hour clock for ease of reading:

Red = 7:00 - 7:59 AM
Orange = 8:00 - 8:59 AM
Yellow = 12:00 (noon) - 2:30 PM
Green = 4:00 - 6:00 PM
Blue = 8:00 - 8:30 PM
Purple = 12:00 (midnight) - 2:00 AM
